**authType** | **String** | Authorization type. Possible values:   - &#x60;AUTOCAPTURE&#x60;: automatic capture.  - &#x60;STANDARDCAPTURE&#x60;: standard capture.  - &#x60;VERBAL&#x60;: forced capture.
